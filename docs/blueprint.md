# **App Name**: EJA Globaltrans Digital

## Core Features:

- Company Profile: Detailed information about EJA Globaltrans, including its history, mission, and values. Address should reflect location in Polígon Industrial Constantí, Tarragona.
- Services Offered: Comprehensive listing of all transport services with clear descriptions and pricing information.
- Real-time Tracking: Track and trace shipments in real-time using tracking numbers.
- Service Request Form: Integrated and easy-to-use form to request new transportation services. Generative AI-powered form-filler tool suggesting form field completions to simplify entry for users.
- Contact & Support: Dedicated contact page with contact form, phone number, email, and address, to offer seamless support for users with a variety of communication preferences.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep ocean blue (#304D6D) to evoke trust, stability, and reliability.
- Background color: Soft light gray (#F0F4F8) to ensure readability and a professional look.
- Accent color: Vivid cyan (#2ACFCF) for CTAs, highlights, and interactive elements.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif font providing a modern, clean, and easily readable experience across all devices.
- Use modern, universally-recognized icons representing logistics, tracking, and services.
- Clean, intuitive layout with clear navigation for easy service booking and information access.
- Subtle transitions and loading animations to provide feedback and enhance user engagement.
